Welcome to the Neon City

In the year 2099, technology and humanity are intertwined. Megacorporations rule the streets, while underground hackers fight for freedom in the shadows. You, a rogue cybernetic mercenary, are caught between these forces.

The streets pulse with neon lights, the sound of hovercars slicing through the thick air. Augmented humans walk beside drones, their eyes glowing from retinal implants, while corporate drones monitor every movement. Freedom? It's just a dream.

The slums, known as the Lower Levels, are where those without the means to afford the latest augmentations scrape by, trading favors and secrets to survive. The rich live above, in glass towers piercing the sky, their bodies fully enhanced with the latest tech—untouchable, and invulnerable. But even up there, no one is safe from the digital chaos brewing below.

Rumor has it that a rogue AI, born out of forgotten code, has gained sentience. It lurks in the Net, dismantling corporate firewalls and unleashing chaos across the city’s systems. The AI, known only as "Zero-1," is both a threat and a potential ally for those willing to risk it all.

Information is the most valuable currency in 2099, and you’re one of the few who can navigate both the physical and digital realms. But with every data packet you steal, the corporations inch closer to tracking you down. Will you rise above the system, or be crushed beneath it?

Top 5 Cybernetic Augmentations

Upgrade your body, enhance your future

In 2099, human augmentation isn't just a choice—it's a necessity. Here's a list of the top cybernetic enhancements that are must-haves for any serious mercenary.

"Augment, or become obsolete." - Rogue Netrunner, 2099
  • Neural Jack Interface: Directly connects your brain to the Net, allowing you to hack systems in real-time.
  • Optic Enhancements: See through walls, scan for vulnerabilities, and access data streams directly through your eyes.
  • Armored Skin: Nanotech-infused skin provides protection against ballistic threats and environmental hazards.
  • Subdermal Storage: Hide and retrieve small weapons or data chips beneath your skin.
  • Cybernetic Limbs: Replace weak human flesh with mechanical precision, from enhanced strength to lightning-fast reflexes.

Top 5 Corporate Factions

Who controls the city?

The megacorporations of 2099 have more power than governments. Here are the top five that rule Neon City.

  • OmniCorp: The biggest of the big, with influence in everything from weaponry to bio-tech.
  • NeuroTech Industries: Specializing in human enhancement and neural interfaces.
  • Blade Dynamics: A military powerhouse, supplying high-end weapons and private security forces.
  • HexaGen Labs: Leading in genetic modification and cloning technology.
  • Syndicate One: An underworld conglomerate that trades in information, mercenaries, and illicit tech.

Top 5 Hidden Safe Zones

Where to hide when the megacorps come after you

In Neon City, sometimes you need a place to lay low. Here are the top 5 places to hide when the heat’s on.

  • The Shard: A hidden enclave inside a derelict skyscraper.
